Structural Directive

  • Structural directives in Angular are a type of directive that can change the structure of the DOM.
  • They are responsible for adding, removing, or manipulating elements in the DOM. Unlike attribute directives, which change the appearance or behavior of an element, structural directives affect the layout by altering the DOM.
  • Some Build in structural directives are
    1. *ngIf
    2. *ngFor
    3. *ngSwitch
